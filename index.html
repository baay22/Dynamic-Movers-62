CHEAP BATHROOM UPGRADES YOU CAN DO YOURSELF

Recently we have talked to Ian who moved from New Jersey to New York City. He had hired some West New York movers to relocate him. After moving in, he and his wife Jessica realized that they had spent too much money, so they didn’t have enough to do the whole refurbishing. In order to save the budget, they incorporated some DIY bathroom upgrades. Here are some of their marvelous ideas that will help you so that you refresh your bathroom in the most inexpensive way.

Benefits of DIY bathroom upgrades

After Ian and Jessica moved in, they looked at their relocation budget checklist. Even though they had found one of the most affordable movers NYC could have, they still lacked money for renovation. Thus, they saw the main advantages of DIY bathroom remodeling:

  • they can be really cheap
  • you can express your creativity
  • through those bathroom upgrades, you get to know the place better
  • they don’t take much time

Bathroom makeover can be a fun activity if you do it with the right person.

Bathroom updates with wallpapers

Peel-and-stick wallpapers are very useful. You can put them on cabinets, shelves, or other items. They come in handy when you want to add or change the monotonous pattern of colors, shapes, and designs. These bathroom updates can be really cheap and don’t require much time. Just don’t exaggerate by covering your bathroom with that wallpaper. You don’t want it to look like a circus.

Mirror frames for a luxurious look

Change or redo the old mirror frame. But, decide on the style and colors first. The type of frame considerably depends on the layout and pattern of the tiles on the wall. Be creative with bathroom remodeling and make use of paint, spray, wood, sticky paper, metal pieces, even parts of tiles, etc.

Bathroom upgrades with new shelves

You can always create more space for your bathroom stuff, such as make-up, shampoo, soap, towels, etc. Get some metal, glass, plastic, or wooden shelves and anchor them into the wall. You can even do some bathroom updates by adding more levels to the existing shelves and cupboards.

Install some new bathroom shelves to use as much space as you can.

Reusing as a way of bathroom remodeling

You don’t have to throw out the old things just to have new ones instead. For instance, why would you buy a new vanity unit when the existing one is fine. If you don’t like that one, you might wish to do a bit of makeover. Try restaining that wooden item with some gel stain. And it is only a three-hour job!

In addition to that, you don’t need to replace the bathtub that looks perfectly fine. Provided there are some yellow stains or similar imperfections, you can easily fix that and your bathtub will be far shinier.

After the updates

When you finish everything, it is time to relax. Then, enjoy your cheap bathroom upgrades with a warm bath. Get a glass of wine and let the sheer pleasure begin.
